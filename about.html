Hi, I'm Hasan —
driven by science and shaped by community.

I'm a freshman at Rowan University majoring in Biochemistry on the pre-medicine track. My goal is to pursue an MD — not just as a career, but as a calling. I'm drawn to medicine because of its unique intersection of rigorous science and genuine human service.

Outside the lab and lecture hall, I'm deeply involved in community. I've coordinated weekly halaqahs, taught at a weekend Islamic school, and taken on leadership roles that sharpen both my organizational skills and my sense of responsibility. This side of me is just as much a part of who I am as the biochemistry.

I describe my approach to work as organized chaos — I thrive in structured environments but stay adaptable when things shift. I'm still figuring out a lot as a freshman, and I embrace that. Curiosity is the engine, discipline is the direction.
